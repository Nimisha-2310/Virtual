Welcome to Punjab

The Land of Vibrance – Culture, Festivals, and Cuisine

Culture & Heritage

Punjab is famous for its lively culture, traditional dances like Bhangra and Gidda, and magnificent historical sites like the Golden Temple and Jallianwala Bagh.

Traditional Cuisine

Punjabi cuisine is rich and flavorful, with dishes like Butter Chicken, Makki di Roti with Sarson da Saag, and sweets like Gajar ka Halwa.

Festivals & Celebrations

Punjab is known for colorful festivals like Baisakhi, Lohri, and Diwali, celebrated with energetic folk dances, music, and grand gatherings.
