Culture & Heritage

Jammu & Kashmir is known for its breathtaking landscapes and unique cultural heritage. From the Mughal gardens of Srinagar to the spiritual Vaishno Devi shrine, the region beautifully blends nature, spirituality, and history.

Traditional Cuisine

Kashmiri cuisine is world-famous, with delicacies like Rogan Josh, Gushtaba, and Kahwa (traditional tea). The rich Wazwan feast is a cultural symbol of hospitality in Kashmir.

Festivals & Celebrations

Festivals like Shikara Festival, Hemis Festival, and Losar showcase Kashmir’s unique traditions, dances, and music amidst snowy landscapes and cultural vibrance.
